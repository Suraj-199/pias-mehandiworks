About

Mehandi Designs are becoming popular once again. In olden days Mehandi was used during some festivals or a marriage, but now-a-day's ladies of all age groups are getting attracted with henna designs with lot of designs to choose starting from Indian Mehandi Designs to Arabic Mehandi Designs.However, these days, mehandi designs have evolved and got a lot of transformation and people prefer to have simpler, yet elegant designs to be drawn on their hands. Arabic and zardozi designs are preferred over patterns that were drawn on the entire hand..

Pia is a professional beautician who has been in the Mehandi design industry for the past 20 years having done various traditional and latest designs based on the changing customer tastes and trends.Her regular customers include wedding parties, participants of stage functions,cultural events etc.These designs are made to match with the dresses, lehengas, or sarees having intrinsic designs. Currently she provide mehandi works in areas in and around Kollam district,Kerala,India.
